Privacy Policy

Post Patcher — Last updated: 2026-06-30

1Introduction

Post Patcher is a personal content management tool that helps publish and schedule posts to social media platforms including Instagram, Threads, and Bluesky. This policy describes how the application handles user data.

2Data We Access

When you connect your accounts, the app accesses only the minimum data required:

  • Instagram / Facebook account name and ID (for posting)
  • Access tokens (stored locally on your device only)
  • Media you explicitly upload for publishing

3Data Storage & Security

All credentials and tokens are stored locally on your machine in environment files and are never transmitted to any external server. No user data is collected, stored on remote servers, or shared with any third party.

4Third-Party Services

This app interacts with the following APIs solely to perform the actions you initiate:

  • Meta Graph API (Instagram / Facebook / Threads)
  • Bluesky AT Protocol

No data is sent to analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5Data Deletion

Since no data is stored remotely, you can delete all local data at any time by removing the application files from your device. To revoke API access, disconnect the app from your Meta account settings.
